Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025  |  Last Updated: 1 June 2026

FOOTBALLHUB ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use the FOOTBALLHUB Portal — our online futsal field booking and management system.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you register and use our system:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, and password (hashed).
  • Booking Data: Field selected, date, time slot, branch, and booking history.
  • Payment Data: Transaction ID, amount paid, payment method (FPX/Online Banking). We do not store your full bank account or card details.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and log data collected automatically when you access our portal.
  • Membership Data: VIP membership tier and subscription dates if applicable.
2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To process and confirm futsal field bookings.
  • To facilitate payment and issue receipts via QR code.
  • To send booking confirmations and system notifications.
  • To manage staff attendance and task assignments (staff users only).
  • To generate performance and revenue reports for managers.
  • To improve our system features and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Malaysian law.
3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: FPX / online banking gateway partners, solely for transaction processing.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Malaysian law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Branch Managers: Booking and user data is accessible to authorised branch managers and staff within the FOOTBALLHUB system for operational purposes.
4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Booking and payment records are retained for a minimum of 7 years for financial compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

5. Cookies & Session Data

Our portal uses session cookies to maintain your login state. These are essential cookies and do not track you across other websites. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Password hashing using BCrypt.
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Role-based access control (Customer, Staff, Manager).
  • Server-side session management via Spring Security.
7. Your Rights

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) Malaysia, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (may affect service availability).
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@footballhub.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. Continued use of the portal after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
