The Vision

Our Legacy

From a single field to a nationwide network — swipe to explore our journey.

Swipe left to right

2015
1 Field Kuala Terengganu

The Seed 🌱

A single piece of land in Kuala Terengganu. No facilities, no lights — just a dream to build something great for the local community.

2017
1 Branch Terengganu

First Field Opens ⚽

FOOTBALLHUB Branch 1 officially opens in Kuala Terengganu. Our very first futsal field welcomes its first players — the community responds with sold-out bookings every weekend.

2019
2 Branches + Kuantan

Expanding East Coast 🗺️

Demand explodes. A second branch opens in Kuantan, Pahang — proving that the FOOTBALLHUB model works beyond our home state. Two cities. One mission.

2021
4 Branches + KL & Johor

Going National 🇲🇾

FOOTBALLHUB crosses state lines into Kuala Lumpur and Johor Bahru. Each branch now features multiple fields — both grass and turf — with professional stadium lighting.

2023
6 Branches + Penang & Selangor

Smart Booking Launches 📱

Our digital portal goes live. Players across all 6 branches can now book fields, pay online, and receive QR-coded receipts — zero phone calls needed.

2025
10+ Branches Nationwide 🇲🇾

Nationwide Network 🏆

FOOTBALLHUB now operates 10+ branches across Malaysia — from Perlis to Johor. Thousands of bookings processed monthly. One field became a movement.

Coming Soon

What's Next? 🚀

Indoor arenas, international partnerships, and AI-powered match analytics. The FOOTBALLHUB story is far from over.
